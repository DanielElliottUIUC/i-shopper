E-Commerce Engineer

Full-Time / Part-Time UIUC Engineering

We're looking for engineers with e-commerce platform experience to help build our marketplace integrations, product search infrastructure, and data pipelines. You'll work on the core systems that power our shopping agent's ability to search across retailers and analyze products at scale.

What you'll do:

  • Build integrations with major e-commerce platforms (Amazon, Shopify, etc.)
  • Design scalable product search and retrieval systems
  • Develop data pipelines for product information and pricing
  • Optimize performance for real-time product comparisons
  • Work with our ML team to serve recommendations at scale

You're a great fit if you have:

  • Experience with e-commerce platforms or marketplace APIs
  • Strong backend engineering skills (Python, Node.js, or Go)
  • Knowledge of web scraping, APIs, and data extraction
  • Understanding of distributed systems and caching strategies
  • Passion for consumer products and user experience

UX Designer - Trust & Shopping Flows

Full-Time / Part-Time UIUC Design

We're seeking a UX designer who understands how to build trust in AI-powered shopping experiences. You'll design the flows that help users feel confident delegating high-stakes purchase decisions to our agent, from product recommendations to checkout experiences.

What you'll do:

  • Design trustworthy AI interaction patterns for shopping decisions
  • Create intuitive flows for product comparison and selection
  • Build verification and transparency features into the agent experience
  • Conduct user research with target customers
  • Design for both web and mobile platforms

You're a great fit if you have:

  • Experience designing e-commerce or shopping experiences
  • Strong portfolio showing user-centered design process
  • Understanding of trust and transparency in AI systems
  • Proficiency with Figma or similar design tools
  • User research and testing experience
  • Bonus: Experience designing conversational interfaces or agents
